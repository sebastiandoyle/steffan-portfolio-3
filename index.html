About

Steffan Doyle is an Australian comedy creator and emerging actor whose character impressions have captured audiences across platforms. His Rio movie bird character work achieved viral success with over 225,000 likes on TikTok, establishing him as a distinctive voice in comedy content creation. With acting credits including 'The Spider' and a growing portfolio of brand collaborations, Steffan bridges digital content and traditional performance.

Based in Australia, Steffan creates comedy content that combines physical performance, voice work, and sharp comedic timing. His multi-platform presence spans TikTok and Instagram, where he continues to build an engaged audience drawn to his character-driven comedy.
